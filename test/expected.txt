Title: Iced Water with Lemon

Directions:
  Remove ice cube tray from freezer.
  Remove ice cubes from tray and add to a small glass.
  Add water to glass.
  Server immediately, garnish with a slice of lemon.

Ingredients:
  4 ice cubes
  1 cup water
  1 slice of lemon

Yield: Makes one cup of iced water.
